Conclusion

Conclusion

The campus population mapping system will utilize two architectures, pipe and filters and Client-Server architecture. These are both commonly used and very reliable architectures, our findings seem to fit the norm. Our ability to aggregate and anonymized data every five minutes makes our website secure and performant, as well as near real time. The visualization of 3D maps and being able to drill into floor plans provides user friendly features to a valuable dataset. Our website combined with the LDAP OAUTH token for security, the streaming of data, and the modern visualizations make the campus population mapping system able to help visual the population density of the Virginia tech campus, helping to limit the spread of COVID-19.
